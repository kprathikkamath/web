 We recognizing the urgent need to preserve its sanctity from the pollution that it is prone to from neighbouring city such as Delhi, have taken up an effort to plan & execute a project that has a potential to enrich the nature , protect the indegeneous cows , various kunds by employing a technique that can parallely generate a source of income to brjvasis (residents of vrndavan) thus preventing the youths from falling into the clutches of urban sprawl. Making the people realise that their old lifestyle of farming , rearing cows ,a simple living with high thinking which all great stalwarts leaders of Sanathana  Dharma like Srila Prabhupada had propounded.
